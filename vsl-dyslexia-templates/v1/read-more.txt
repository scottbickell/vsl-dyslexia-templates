It’s Dyslexia:
What’s a Teacher to Do?

By Dr. Louisa Moats

What is Dyslexia? Dyslexia means, by its Greek roots, difficulty with words. More specifically, dyslexia is an unexpected problem with accurate and efficient recognition and spelling of printed words. It is to be distinguished from reading comprehension problems that may occur even though students can read the words reasonably well.
